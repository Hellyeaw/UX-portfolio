Jewelery &
Textiles

I always loved accessorizing my outfits, I believe jewelery can higlight the beautiful features of the body and an outfit. However, it can also be costly. One day when I was at work, I saw a customer with very uniqu earings and when I asked her where she got them from, she told me she made them and that I should try making my own too. So as I went home I took out the old jewelery that I was no longer using and found a long wire from my arts and crafts box. And, that is how I started making my own jewelery for the past couple of years. I eventually started using old fabrics, stones and clay in my designs.
